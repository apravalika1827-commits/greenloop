About Us

GreenLoop is a student-driven venture that enables easy e-waste collection and recycling inside college campuses. We help students dispose of old chargers, headphones, laptops, and more through a simple app or portal. Our mission is to promote eco-friendly practices and reduce toxic e-waste from harming the environment.

How It Works

  1. Login to our app or web portal
  2. Schedule a pickup or locate a nearby drop-off point
  3. We collect the items every week
  4. Items are sent to authorized recyclers
